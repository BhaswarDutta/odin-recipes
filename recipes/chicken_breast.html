Chicken Breast

Juicy, golden-brown chicken breast is a versatile and healthy protein option that pairs well with almost any side. Perfectly seared and simply seasoned, this dish delivers flavor without fuss.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tbsp olive oil (or unsalted butter)
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup green peas (steamed or boiled)
  • 1/4 cup chopped pickles (or avocado, depending on taste)
  • 5-6 cherry tomatoes, halved

Instructions

  1. Place the chicken breasts between plastic wrap or parchment and pound them to an even thickness (about 3/4 inch) for even cooking.
  2. Pat the chicken dry with a paper towel. Rub both sides with olive oil and season with salt, pepper, smoked paprika, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  3. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat. Once hot, add 1 tbsp of olive oil (or butter) and let it heat until shimmering.
  4. Place the chicken in the pan and cook for 5-6 minutes on the first side without moving it. This allows a nice golden crust to form.
  5. Flip the chicken and cook for another 5-6 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 74°C (165°F).
  6. In the last 2 minutes, add a small pat of butter and spoon it over the chicken while it finishes cooking.
  7. Transfer the chicken to a plate and let it rest for 5 minutes before slicing to lock in the juices.
  8. While the chicken rests, boil or steam the peas, chop the pickles (or avocado), and halve the cherry tomatoes.
  9. Slice one chicken breast and leave the other whole. Serve with peas, pickles/avocado, and cherry tomatoes. Add mustard and BBQ sauce on the side.
