Caramel Bliss Pancakes

Fluffy stack of pancakes topped with fresh strawberries, caramel sauce, and candied nuts. Served with a side of whipped cream and a light dusting of powdered sugar.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Granulated sugar
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Unsalted butter (melted)
  • Fresh strawberries (sliced)
  • Caramel sauce
  • Candied nuts (e.g., walnuts or pecans)
  • Powdered sugar
  • Whipped cream
  • Fresh mint (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. In another bowl, mix milk, eggs, and melted butter until well combined.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Do not overmix.
  4. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and lightly grease it with butter or oil.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface and the edges look set, about 2-3 minutes.
  7. Flip and cook the other side until golden brown, about 1-2 minutes more.
  8. Stack the cooked pancakes on a plate.
  9. Drizzle warm caramel sauce generously over the pancake stack.
  10. Top with sliced strawberries and candied nuts.
  11. Sprinkle with powdered sugar and garnish with a fresh mint leaf.
  12. Serve with a side of whipped cream and enjoy!
