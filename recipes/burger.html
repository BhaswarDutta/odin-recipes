Chicken Burger

This burger is packed with flavor, featuring a tender chicken patty, melted cheese, crisp lettuce, fresh tomatoes, and a golden sesame seed bun, perfect for a gourmet-style meal at home.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground chicken (minced)
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce or soy sauce
  • 4 burger buns (preferably brioche or sesame seed buns)
  • 4 cheddar cheese slices
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1 small red onion, sliced
  • Leafy lettuce or romaine
  • Mayonnaise
  • Butter (for toasting buns)
  • Pickles (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken Patty: In a large bowl, mix the ground chicken, breadcrumbs, onion, garlic, egg, pepper, paprika, chili flakes, salt, and Worcestershire sauce.
  2. Mix thoroughly until combined.
  3. Divide into 4 equal parts and shape into round patties slightly wider than your buns.
  4. Place on a plate, cover, and refrigerate for 15-20 minutes to help them firm up.
  5. Cook the Patties: Heat a nonstick skillet or grill pan over medium heat and add a little oil.
  6. Cook the patties for 5-6 minutes per side, until golden brown and cooked through (internal temperature should be 75°C or 165°F).
  7. In the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheddar on each patty to melt.
  8. Prepare the Buns: Slice the buns in half and lightly butter the insides.
  9. Toast them on a hot pan or grill until golden brown.
  10. Assemble the Burger: Spread mayonnaise on the bottom bun.
  11. Place the cheesy chicken patty on the bun.
  12. Add tomato slices, red onion, and lettuce.
  13. Top with pickles (optional).
  14. Finish with the top bun and secure with a skewer if needed.
